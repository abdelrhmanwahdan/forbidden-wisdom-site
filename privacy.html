Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Forbidden Wisdom handles information in connection with our content distribution tool and social media presence.

1. Information We Collect

Our automated content posting tool connects to social media platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook) using OAuth2 authorization. We store only the OAuth tokens required to post content on behalf of our own accounts. We do not collect, store, or process any data from viewers or followers.

2. How We Use Information

OAuth tokens are used solely to publish educational video content to our social media accounts. Tokens are stored securely and are never shared with third parties.

3. TikTok API Usage

We use TikTok's Content Posting API to publish videos to our official Forbidden Wisdom TikTok account. We access only the following scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to verify account identity
  • video.upload — to post educational video content

We do not access, collect, or store any data from TikTok users other than our own account.

4. Data Storage

API credentials are stored securely using GitHub Actions Secrets (encrypted at rest). No viewer or follower data is ever stored.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. API credentials are never shared outside of our secure automated systems.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate our content publishing system. Tokens can be revoked at any time via the respective platform's account settings.

7. Your Rights

As a viewer, you are not required to provide any personal data to access our content. Your use of TikTok, YouTube, or other platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
